# **Physical Activity Levels May Impact on the Risk of Premature Mortality in People with Epilepsy**

## Overview

This repository contains the data and analysis related to the study on how physical activity levels may impact the risk of premature mortality in individuals with epilepsy. The study aims to provide insights into potential lifestyle modifications that could improve health outcomes for people living with epilepsy.
